# Support

- **Questions / ideas:** open a [Discussion](https://github.com/rnwolfe/vabc/discussions).
- **Bugs / feature requests:** open an [Issue](https://github.com/rnwolfe/vabc/issues/new/choose).
- **Security:** see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) — report privately, not via a public issue.

Before filing a bug, run `vabc doctor --online` and include its output, your `vabc --version`,
and your OS.
